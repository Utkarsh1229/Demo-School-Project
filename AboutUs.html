About Us

History

Founded in 1980 , the school is a venture of the “ABC Educational Trust”. Since it’s inception , the school has grown and established a reputation for incorporating innovative and modern methods of the teaching and learning process; not to mention academic excellence.


The school is nested amidst 25 acres of lush green environs offering its students a first hand experience with nature; thus providing an opportunity for “interactive learning”.

Defining moments of XYZ senior secondary school in India

  • We take great pride on being awarded the India's Best Chain of Senior Secondary Schools Award from 2008-2012. Been awarded the Best Infrastructure Award 2015 – 16.

  • Awarded India’s Top 20 Day cum Boarding School Award.

  • Awarded Innovation in Early Learning in 2017-18.

  • XYZ Senior Secondary School is proud of it’s little champs who have won Gold & laurels at various National and State Level Athletic Meets.

  • State of the art infrastructure for both Sports and other Co-curricular activities.

Vision

To provide quality, comprehensive education and raise compassionate global citizens.

Mission

To facilitate life-long learning beyond knowledge acquisition by providing them skills to comprehend, apply, appreciate, analyze, synthesize and evaluate.

Motto and Slogan

XYZ Senior Secondary School is a nurturing home for children from all walks of life the world-over, where each child’s multiple skills are sharpened to the zenith.

We strive to give them a conducive environment and a value enriched education,while nurturing them into responsible and confident individuals who will make an effective contribution to the society.
